Thank you for installing The Lost dimension mod!

This mod introduces a variety of endgame content, including:
10+ new hostile mobs
1 new passive mob
9+ new bosses
3+ tiers of dungeons
A LOT of rooms to ensure unique dungeon experiences
A LOT of loot, some of which is randomized
1 new overworld structure that provides access to all of the other content
